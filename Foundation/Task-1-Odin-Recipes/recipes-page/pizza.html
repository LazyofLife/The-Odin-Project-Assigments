Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni Pizza is an all-time favorite Italian-American dish, featuring a chewy-crisp crust topped with tangy tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella cheese, and slices of spicy pepperoni. It’s simple, satisfying, and perfect for both casual dinners and special gatherings.

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 packet (7 g) instant dry yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water (about 40°C / 105°F)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

For the Sauce:

  • 1 1/2 cups canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

For the Topping:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 100-150 g pepperoni slices
  • 2 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Fresh basil leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    1. In a large bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it rest for 5-10 minutes until foamy
    2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms
    3. Knead for 8-10 minutes on a floured surface until smooth and elastic
    4. Place in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise for 1–2 hours until doubled in size
  2. Make the Sauce:
    1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat. Sauté garlic until fragrant
    2. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, basil, sugar, salt, and pepper
    3. Simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let cool slightly
  3. Preheat the Oven:
    1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F) or as hot as it will go. If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating
  4. Shape the Dough:
    1. Punch down the risen dough and divide if making smaller pizzas
    2. Roll or stretch into a 12-inch circle on a lightly floured surface. Transfer to a baking sheet or hot pizza stone
  5. Assemble the Pizza:
    1. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough
    2. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly
    3. Arrange pepperoni slices on top. Add Parmesan if desired
  6. Bake:
    1. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned
  7. Serve:
    1. Remove from oven, garnish with fresh basil if desired, slice, and serve hot
