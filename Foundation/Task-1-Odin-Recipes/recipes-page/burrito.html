Beef and Bean Burrito

Description

A burrito is a Mexican-American favorite made by wrapping a soft flour tortilla around a hearty filling. This version features seasoned ground beef, refried beans, rice, cheese, and fresh toppings, all rolled into a warm tortilla. It's satisfying, customizable, and great for both quick meals and family dinners.

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 g (1 lb) ground beef
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground oregano
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 1 cup refried beans
  • 1 cup cooked rice

For Assembly:

  • 4 large flour tortillas (10-12 inch)
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 cup salsa or pico de gallo
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • Fresh cilantro (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Beef Filling:
    1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat
    2. Add onion and cook until soft, about 3 minutes
    3. Stir in garlic, then add ground beef. Cook until browned, breaking it into small pieces
    4. Season with cumin, smoked paprika, chili powder, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix well and cook for another 2-3 minutes
  2. Warm the Tortillas:
    1. Heat each tortilla in a dry skillet for 30 seconds per side or wrap them in foil and warm in the oven at 150°C (300°F) for 5-7 minutes
  3. Assemble the Burritos:
    1. Lay one tortilla flat. Spread a spoonful of refried beans in the center
    2. Add a scoop of rice, followed by beef filling
    3. Sprinkle generously with shredded cheese
    4. Top with salsa, sour cream, lettuce, and tomatoes
  4. Fold the Burrito:
    1. Fold in the sides of the tortilla, then roll it tightly from the bottom up to enclose the filling
  5. Optional Toasting:
    1. For a crispy exterior, place the rolled burrito seam-side down in a skillet and toast for 1-2 minutes per side until golden brown
  6. Serve:
    1. Slice in half if desired, garnish with cilantro, and serve with extra salsa and sour cream on the side
