Chicken Doner Kebab

Description

A delicious chicken doner kebab with fresh vegetables, inspired by the famous Turkish street food. Tender, marinated chicken is cooked to perfection and served inside warm flatbread or pita, paired with crisp vegetables and a creamy garlic-yogurt sauce. Itâ€™s flavorful, satisfying, and perfect for lunch, dinner, or a weekend feast.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500 g (1 lb) boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • 3 tbsp plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice>
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder (optional, for heat)
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp mayonnaise (optional, for creaminess)
  • 1 clove garlic, finely grated
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

For Assembly:

  • 4 flatbreads or pita bread, warmed
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 medium cucumber, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium tomato, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 small red onion, thinly sliced
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, and spices
    2. Add chicken thighs, coat well, and marinate at least 1 hour (or overnight for best flavor)
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    1. Heat a grill pan, skillet, or outdoor grill over medium-high heat
    2. Cook chicken for 5-6 minutes per side until fully cooked and slightly charred
    3. Rest for 5 minutes, then slice into thin strips
  3. Prepare the Sauce:
    1. In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt, mayonnaise (if using), garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper until smooth.
  4. Assemble the Kebab:
    1. Place warm flatbread on a plate
    2. Spread a spoonful of yogurt sauce down the center
    3. Layer lettuce, cucumber, tomato, and onion
    4. Add sliced chicken on top
    5. Drizzle with extra sauce and sprinkle parsley if desired
  5. Serve:
    1. Fold the bread over the filling and wrap in parchment or foil for easy eating
    2. Serve immediately with extra sauce on the side
